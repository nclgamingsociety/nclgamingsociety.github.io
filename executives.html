As President, Angela is responsible for managing the whole executive team and assisting with tasks as needed. She also has the final say in some big decisions, and is our spokesperson when communicating with other societies/companies.

As Treasurer, Olly manages the society's finances, and produces our annual budget. He also buys the prizes for our special events such as quizzes!

As Secretary, Katy manages our emails and the general administration of our society. She provides a list of members to the Student Union and emails all our members about upcoming events! She also books rooms for our society's social events.

As Tech Officer, Ollie manages the society's equipment. He safely stores all of our consoles used in weekly meets and brings them to the meets for society members to use. He also helps set up consoles for any special events with the help of the esports officers.

As Social & Welfare Officer, Lauren manages our social media, such as Facebook, Twitter and Twitch and posts regular updates about what's been happening in the society! If you ever see a post on any of these, it was probably her.

As the Esports Officer for Overwatch, Oscar has to manage the 3 Overwatch teams we currently have and organise scrims/practice sessions for them! All of the esports officers help organise the society's gaming competitions alongside the Tech Officer.

As the Esports Officer for League of Legends, Jamie has to manage the 1 League of Legends team we have currently and organise scrims/practice sessions for them! All of the esports officers help organise the society's gaming competitions alongside the Tech Officer.

As the Esports Officer for Counter Strike: Global Offensive, Ed has to manage the 1 Counter Strike: Global Offensive team we have currently and organise scrims/practice sessions for them! All of the esports officers help organise the society's gaming competitions alongside the Tech Officer.
