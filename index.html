About Us

Welcome to the official Newcastle University Gaming Society webpage! We are a friendly and inclusive society of 150+ members for all things gaming! We host weekly meets every Friday for our members to hang out and play the latest games together, and host seasonal quizzes made by our executive team! We also have esports teams that represent our university in leagues with other universities across the country. Join our active discord community and check out our other content down below.
